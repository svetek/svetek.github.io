# Enroll an Android Phone with a Work Profile

Use this guide to enroll a personal Android phone for company access. Enrollment creates a separate **Work** profile on your phone. Company apps and company data stay in the Work profile. Your personal apps, photos, text messages, and personal data remain separate.

## Before You Start

Make sure you have:

- Your company email address and password.
- Access to your MFA method.
- A supported Android phone with Google Play services.
- Chrome or Microsoft Edge installed.
- A stable Wi-Fi or cellular connection.

Do not start enrollment from Android Private Space or a secondary phone user profile.

## Start Enrollment

Use one of these options:

- Open Chrome or Microsoft Edge and go to `https://aka.ms/enrollmyandroid`.
- Open Microsoft Outlook and sign in with your company account.
- Open Microsoft Teams and sign in with your company account.

If Outlook or Teams says your organization requires the device to be managed, follow the prompts to continue enrollment.

## Sign In

1. Sign in with your company email address.
2. Complete MFA when prompted.
3. Follow the setup prompts.
4. If the browser asks to add your work account to the browser, skip that prompt and continue enrollment.

## Create the Work Profile

Android will guide you through creating the Work profile.

During setup:

1. Review the work profile information.
2. Accept the prompts to create the Work profile.
3. Wait while Android installs required work apps.
4. Keep the phone unlocked and connected until setup completes.

You may see apps such as **Microsoft Intune**, **Company Portal**, **Authenticator**, **Outlook**, or **Teams** install in the Work profile.

## Find Your Work Apps

After enrollment, your phone will show personal apps and work apps separately.

Depending on your Android version and phone manufacturer, work apps may appear:

- In a **Work** tab in the app drawer.
- With a small briefcase icon on the app.
- In a separate **Work** section.

Open work apps from the Work profile when accessing company email, files, or chats.

## Verify Enrollment

Enrollment is complete when:

- A Work profile exists on the phone.
- Work apps show a briefcase icon or appear in the Work tab.
- Outlook or Teams opens without asking you to enroll again.
- Company Portal or Microsoft Intune shows the device as compliant.

## Common Issues

| Issue | What to do |
| --- | --- |
| Enrollment does not start | Open Chrome or Edge and go to `https://aka.ms/enrollmyandroid` |
| MFA fails | Confirm your MFA method works, then try again |
| Work apps are missing | Wait a few minutes, then open the managed Play Store or Company Portal |
| Outlook still asks to enroll | Open the Outlook app from the Work profile, not the personal profile |
| Setup gets stuck | Restart the phone and try the enrollment link again |
| You see two app icons | Use the app with the briefcase icon for company data |

## Remove the Work Profile

Only remove the Work profile if IT asks you to or if you are leaving the company.

Removing the Work profile deletes company apps and company data from your phone. It does not delete personal apps, photos, text messages, or personal data.

## Need Help

Contact your IT support team if enrollment fails, your phone does not create a Work profile, or company apps still ask you to enroll after setup is complete.
