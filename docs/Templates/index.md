Use these templates as starting points for client communication, operational documentation, and repeatable rollout workflows. Replace bracketed placeholders before sending or storing a client-ready version.

## Template Categories

| Category | Use |
| --- | --- |
| [UniFi Templates](UniFi/) | UniFi Access, Protect, Network, Endpoint, and Console rollout or operations templates |
| [10DLC Templates](10DLC/SMS_Policy_Example/) | SMS/text messaging policy examples |

## Publishing Notes

- Keep templates generic and free of client-specific names, tenant IDs, door names, access codes, phone numbers, or credentials.
- Use bracketed placeholders such as `[ADDRESS]`, `[DATE]`, and `[PHONE]`.
- Publish product families as subcategories so field teams can find the right packet quickly.
- Keep client-filled copies in the client's documentation area, not in this public repository.
